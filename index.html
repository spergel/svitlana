About

Svitlana Mayboroda is a mathematician whose work focuses on analysis and partial differential equations, with applications to wave propagation, disordered media, and quantum systems.

She is a Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics, ETH Zurich and a McKnight Presidential Professor in the School of Mathematics, University of Minnesota .

Research & Leadership

Svitlana Mayboroda is the Director of the Simons Collaboration on Localization of Waves .

Her research has been supported in part by the Simons Collaborations in Mathematics and Physical Sciences and the NSF RAISE–TAQS program, including The Hidden Structure of the Disorder in Quantum Systems (DMS 1839077).
